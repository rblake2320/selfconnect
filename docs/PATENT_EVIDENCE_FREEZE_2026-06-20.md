## Composition Claim

The strongest SelfConnect claim is the composition, not a generic Win32,
desktop automation, IPC, or queueing primitive:

- OS-native role and target identity;
- fail-closed target-fact verification before actuation;
- surface-specific native channel routing;
- echo-filtered verified readback;
- birth ID / generation freshness;
- optional governed role lease using local OS identity;
- tamper-evident event history;
- optional MCP/API adapters that are not required for local actuation.

Representative Windows species already proven or documented:

- terminal path: `WM_CHAR` / UIA readback;
- browser path: UIA `ValuePattern` / `InvokePattern`;
- control-plane identity: named-pipe SID lease;
- readback integrity: nonce/hash echo filter;
- target safety: HWND/PID/exe/class/title guard.

## Durable Virtual Role

The durable virtual role family covers model participants that do not have a
stable visible window:

- `hwnd=0` or no current window;
- durable mailbox;
- `birth_id`;
- generation counter;
- role/capability profile;
- participation in the same mesh evidence history.

This preserves identity for stateless local inference processes, remote model
workers, or logical agents used in benchmark load.

## Execution Hierarchy

Do not claim a rigid ordered script. The claim target is lowest-cost viable
execution-path selection with verified postconditions under a shared
identity/evidence policy.

Representative paths:

1. deterministic replay for known tasks;
2. native APIs when explicitly allowed;
3. Win32/UIA semantic execution;
4. cached scene graph;
5. local OCR/vision;
6. full multimodal reasoning;
7. mouse/keyboard fallback.

The patent-safe framing is selection and verification, not "always perform step
1, then step 2, then step 3."

## MCP/HTTP Competitor Contrast

This is the competitor contrast that belongs in the freeze packet:

- UiPath-style conductor/server orchestration uses application-layer authority
  and tool/robot dispatch.
- Anthropic-style computer use is primarily visual/pixel inference and action.
- Google/browser-agent paths often depend on DOM, extension, CDP, or WebDriver
  access.
- Microsoft ODR/MCP-style governance lives at a connector/platform layer.
- Lancelot/UAB publicly claims a local bridge, MCP tools, API-key localhost
  auth, CDP/Electron/framework hooks, and broad app-control cascade.

SelfConnect's lane is different:

```text
Local OS-native AI peer mesh where authority can be derived from OS/hardware
identity, target facts are checked live, actions route by surface-native channel,
and delivery is verified by echo-filtered readback.
```
